The Challenge

Frailty is accelerating across Hong Kong’s ageing population

Adults over 65 will make up 36% of Hong Kong by 2046, yet frailty screening remains reactive and fragmented, often happening only after falls, hospitalisations, or cognitive decline (Census and Statistics Department, 2023).

Under-detected risk

Clinical frailty tools like the CFS and Fried phenotype take time and can feel stigmatising. Busy primary care clinics lack continuous monitoring, leaving a silent progression window.

Fragmented wellness data

Wearables, mood check-ins, and community participation data sit in silos. Without integration, clinicians and caregivers miss the holistic picture.

Reversible—but missed

Frailty is dynamic and responsive to targeted interventions in strength, nutrition, and engagement. Catching decline sooner keeps elders independent.

Our Solution

ResiliSense adapts to each person in the care circle

A single biosocial intelligence layer powers distinct experiences for older adults and for caregivers & clinicians, meeting each group where they are while syncing data across the ecosystem.

Featured Experience

Personal Vitality Dashboard for Older Adults

A calming, high-contrast dashboard that translates HRV, tremor, gait and activity into clear daily streaks and recovery guidance. Designed with large tap targets, legible typography, and delightful micro-animations for confidence at any age.

ResiliSense · Older Adult View
Cognitive Companion

Voice-first mental wellness check-ins

Multilingual conversations run adaptive recall games, PHQ-9 / GAD-7 screens, and tone tracking to surface changes long before crisis hits.

Longevity Journeys

Hyper-personalised movement & nutrition plans

AI co-designed with geriatricians calibrates strength, balance, and dietary goals— celebrating wins with badges sharable with family circles.

Social Vitality

Community discovery & isolation alerts

Local hobby meetups, district health centre events, and NGO programmes are surfaced with accessibility filters; isolation triggers prompt outreach.

AI Advantage

Responsible AI that adapts to each elder’s journey

01 · Data Fusion

Biosocial signal lake

Normalises wearable, chatbot, and social inputs. Clinical Frailty Scale labels anchor supervised learning, supplemented by self-reported questionnaires.

02 · Predict

Proactive risk scoring

Gradient boosting and sequential deep models forecast frailty change over 30, 90, and 180 day windows, highlighting the most modifiable factors for each individual.

03 · Coach

Personalised interventions

LLM-powered coaching co-designed with geriatricians offers progressive resistance plans, nutrition nudges, and social goals, while respecting cultural nuance.

04 · Safeguard

Ethical governance

Differential privacy, consent management, and human-in-the-loop review ensure transparency and safety, following guidelines from leading AI governance frameworks (Scale AI).

Ecosystem Impact

Reducing frailty burden across stakeholders

ResiliSense unifies data silos and equips each partner to intervene earlier and more empathetically, shifting from treatment to prevention.

Older adults & families

  • Stay independent longer through daily nudges and positive reinforcement.
  • Understand personal risk with accessible visualisations.
  • Invite caregivers to co-manage goals and alerts.

Clinicians

  • Proactively triage using dynamic CFS-aligned risk scoring.
  • Adjust care plans with wearable-contextualised insights.
  • Streamline follow-up through telehealth-ready reporting.

Community partners

  • Target social prescribing to those experiencing isolation.
  • Measure programme impact with aggregated, anonymised trends.
  • Trigger crisis escalation workflows with configurable rules.
